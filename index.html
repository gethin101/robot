First sketch

We first sketched out the design of our robot in terms of components and general hardware placement.

This layout changed later on but it was a good starting point as it allowed us to visualise what setup we wanted and how it would realistically all come together.

List of components:

Raspberry Pi Pico 2 WH - Processes commands & controls the robot

TT Motors x4 - Provides movement to the wheels

L298N Motor Drivers x2 - Controls speed & power of the motors

Servo Motor - Used to move the axe to specific angles

433mhz Reciever - Recieve wireless signals from the transmitter

6xAA Battery pack - Provides power to all the electronics

Breadboard - Connection point for all the components

Jumper Wires - Connects components together

PLA Axe - Main weapon for the robot

Note: We used a breadboard so we could quickly make changes without having to solder.
